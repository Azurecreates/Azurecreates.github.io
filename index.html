About

Regina Ramos is a digital artist, oracle card reader, and animation student. She likes to relate her work with spirituality and personal growth. She is currently studying a Bachelor of Digital Arts at Tecnológico de Monterrey, with a minor in concept art.
When she's not working on school projects, she works on her webcomics, illustrations and freelance illustration.
She was the director, character designer, animator and VFX artist of Máscaras, her first student animated short film.
Whenever she is not drawing or animating she likes to read fantasy novels, play video games,dance, and watch anime.
Recently she started streaming on Twitch.
